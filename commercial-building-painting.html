The Highest Prep & Clean Up Standards & Practices

Your facility is assessed and prepared by a team of experts following industry best practices to ensure optimal adhesion & performance of the paint. We also fully ensure that the work area is established and maintained to be clean, orderly, and liability-free and to protect all surfaces from drips, splatters & spills.

With the unpredictable climate and the change of seasons, it is important to protect your facility from the outdoor elements.

To make sure your facility is fully protected, we thoroughly prepare all surfaces. This can include power washing, caulking cracks, power tool cleaning of flaking paint and rust, patching holes, or even replacing damaged surfaces.

Clean-up: At completion of work each day, we remove all materials, supplies, debris and trash and leave work spaces and paint storage areas in a clean, orderly & safe condition.

Detailed Attention to Safety & Regulations

All Southeast Commercial Services commercial projects are completed with the utmost attention to job site safety, quality & professionalism.

We strictly adhere to OSHA requirements and the application of finishes in accordance with the manufacturer’s recommendations.

We ensure that all materials comply with all current requirements of the Environmental Agency, the appropriate Air Pollution Control District, and all other local, state, or Federal agencies.

We store all equipment & materials in strict compliance with local health and fire regulations.

Disposal of all waste water and unused materials follow current A.Q.M.D. and local legal requirements and regulations.

Painting and preparation of any existing coatings or substrates containing lead are conducted in full compliance with current regulations as defined by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (USEPA).

Southeast carries all the proper licensing, liability & workmans comp insurance & adheres to manufacturer & industry best practices to ensure a high quality & professional result every time.
