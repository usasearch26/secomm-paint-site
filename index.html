What’s Included in a Commercial Painting Scope?

A proper commercial painting quote should include measurable quantities, substrate-specific preparation, coating systems, protection details, scheduling coordination, and documented closeout standards.

Measured Surface Quantities

Square footage (SF), linear footage (LF), door counts, overhead doors, fascia, gutters, downspouts, handrails, bollards, and specialty items clearly itemized.

Substrate-Specific Preparation

Power washing, scraping, sanding, mechanical grinding where necessary, caulking, priming, efflorescence removal, and surface repair coordination.

Manufacturer-Specified Systems

Sherwin-Williams or PPG systems applied per technical data sheets, including wet film and dry film thickness requirements.

Protection & Tenant Coordination

Protection of adjacent areas, landscaping, vehicles, hardware removal, noise coordination, lift access planning, and phased scheduling.

Scheduling & Pre-Commencement Planning

Pre-job meetings, work notifications, coordination with property management, and documented project sequencing.

Inspection & Warranty Alignment

Final walkthrough, punch list completion, workmanship review, and warranty documentation aligned with product selection.

Frequently Asked Questions About Atlanta Commercial Painting Contractors

What's included in a commercial painting quote?

A commercial quote should specify prep steps, coating system, number of coats, protection/containment, schedule, exclusions, and closeout expectations. We walk the site, confirm requirements, and provide a detailed estimate.

How do you minimize disruption in occupied buildings?

By sequencing areas, protecting floors and fixtures, controlling dust and overspray, coordinating access, and scheduling work around operating hours when needed.

Why does surface prep matter so much on commercial projects?

Prep determines adhesion and lifespan. Cleaning, repairs, priming, and contaminant removal are often the difference between "looks good for six months" and "lasts for years."

Can you handle multi-story or high-rise commercial painting?

Yes. High-access work requires planning, staging, and strict safety practices to protect people, property, and timelines. We have the equipment and experience.

Do you offer interior and exterior commercial painting in Atlanta?

Yes. Southeast Commercial Services performs both interior and exterior commercial painting across the Greater Atlanta area.

Do you do residential house painting?

No. We're commercial painting contractors focused on commercial and industrial facilities. We don't paint homes.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Greater Atlanta metro area, including Atlanta, Decatur, Marietta, Alpharetta, Sandy Springs, Buckhead, Midtown, Norcross, Duluth, Suwanee, Lawrenceville, Roswell, Dunwoody, and surrounding communities.

How long does a commercial painting project take?

It depends on the size, scope, and access. A small office might take a few days. A multi-story building could take weeks. We provide a realistic timeline in your estimate and stick to it.

Do you work nights and weekends?

Yes. We can schedule work around your business hours, including nights and weekends, to minimize disruption to your operations.

Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?

Yes. Southeast Commercial Services is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We've been serving Greater Atlanta since 1991.
