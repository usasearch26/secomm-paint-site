SMS Messaging Program — Commercial & Industrial Painting Services
Southeast Commercial Services of Georgia LLC

Southeast Commercial Services of Georgia LLC operates an SMS appointment reminder program for B2B commercial and industrial painting clients. Text STOP to opt out at any time.

SMS Program Description

Southeast Commercial Services of Georgia LLC offers an SMS notification service to keep commercial and industrial painting clients informed about their scheduled appointments. Enrolled participants will receive appointment reminders and confirmations related to their active painting projects and service agreements.

This program is operated exclusively for B2B commercial and industrial clients. Residential or consumer inquiries are not supported through this platform.

How to Opt Out

You may cancel your SMS subscription at any time. To unsubscribe, simply reply with the keyword:

STOP

Upon receipt of your STOP message, we will send a final confirmation SMS verifying that you have been successfully unsubscribed. After that confirmation, you will no longer receive SMS messages from Southeast Commercial Services of Georgia LLC through this program.

To re-enroll in the program in the future, simply sign up again using the same method you used initially, and SMS communications will resume.
