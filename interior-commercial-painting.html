Our Interior Commercial Painting Process

Protect the Area:

We begin our interior jobs by taking the time to protect your facility, equipment, and inventory. All surfaces will be properly prepared before applying the specified coating. Smoke detectors and sprinkler heads are masked or shut down in accordance with building and local fire codes. Signs and barriers are used to direct building occupants and guests safely around the work site. Our crew covers your floors with drop cloths and masks all fixtures, hinges, and doorknobs to shield them from dust and paint drips.

Prepare for Painting:

Preparations include hand and power tool cleaning, patching, priming, filling nail holes, fixing cracks, and stripping old wall coverings. We also repair plaster cracks and caulk gaps where trim molding meets walls and ceilings. Dirt and dust are cleaned off surfaces to improve paint adhesion, and uneven surfaces are sanded to ensure a smooth finish. We also take additional steps to ensure the proper ventilation of the work areas and minimize fumes.

Painting & Coatings:

Our commercial painting teams apply the right primers and finishes to withstand the demands of your business. You can have it all from basic wall coatings to industrial epoxies and everything in between.
