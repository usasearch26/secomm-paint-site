A Professional Process - Not Just a Crew With Brushes

Our Atlanta office painting process includes a documented site walkthrough, surface preparation, furniture and floor protection, low-VOC manufacturer-specified coatings, and phased scheduling by floor or suite to keep your team working throughout the project.

Office painting isn't about slapping paint on walls. It's about planning, prep, protection, and execution that doesn't disrupt your team, your clients, or your operations.

1

Walkthrough

We confirm surfaces, access, office hours, protection needs, and phasing requirements by floor or suite.

2

Surface Prep

Cleaning, patching, sanding, caulking, priming, and repairs—so coatings bond and perform in high-traffic office environments.

3

Protection

We protect flooring, furniture, workstations, and adjacent surfaces from drips, dust, and overspray.

4

Application

Low-VOC roller and spray methods following manufacturer specs for each office coating system.

5

Clean-Up

Materials secured, work areas left orderly, and final punch completed to your standards before your team returns.
